Hello and welcome to Misty’s Adventures!

“Hi, I’m Misty and I’m 6 years old.  For those of you who don’t know me, my auntie is Gina Phillips from the book Introspection.

I love to go on adventures!  My favorite thing is to tell a story of the things that are happening in different people’s lives, because we all must do something positive.  There are bullies out there.  There are things that happen to us, but we can’t give up!  I have some adventures that I hope you’ll find fun!  You can also check out my Auntie Gina in Introspection.  You can meet my cousins Junebug and Jake and my many friends.  I have a close family and we love each other.  That’s why I think you’ll love my adventures!

This adventure is called Kyle’s Kite.  Kyle is a very, very strong-willed boy who loves kites so much that he wanted to make one of his own.  I’m not going to tell you the whole story, I’ll let you read it.  Have fun and enjoy!
