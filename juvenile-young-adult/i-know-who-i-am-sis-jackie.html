Its hard to do the right thing about everything all the time. You are human. You make mistakes. Did you know Paul from your Bible stories had a hard time convincing Christians he no longer wanted to kill them because he had Jesus in his heart? What is your purpose? You might feel too young for God to use. So you know what the right thing to do is, but only do the bare minimum. Meet Lydia and walk through her life while she learns who she is. Change can be a good thing.
