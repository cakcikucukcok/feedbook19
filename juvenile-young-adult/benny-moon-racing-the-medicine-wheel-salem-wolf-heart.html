Deep in the woods, Benny is befriended by an owl who takes him on a magical adventure around the Native American Medicine Wheel. With a touch of Owl’s wing, Benny flies off to meet his animal spirit guides.

An Eagle, a Coyote, a Bear and a Buffalo help Benny solve problems and cope with feelings of loneliness, disappointment and anger. Join Benny as he races the Medicine Wheel and learns how to win—on the track and in his life.
