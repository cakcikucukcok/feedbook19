Sarah is a pretty black cat who loves cheese. She loves all kinds of cheese! Sarah Cat is willing to face any ordeal or take any risk for a piece of cheese! She will torment a nest of bees, allow her tail to be pulled, dance a jig, or even sail the Seven Seas, all just for a piece of cheese! There really isn't anything this kitty won't do for a nice big piece of cheese! Sarah Cat loves cheese so much she even named her ship, THE S.S. CHEESE DIP! Sarah Cat won't even go to sleep at night unless her bear, Ted E. Cheese, is by her side! As a matter of fact, Sarah Cat spends every waking moment just looking for ways to get more cheese.
