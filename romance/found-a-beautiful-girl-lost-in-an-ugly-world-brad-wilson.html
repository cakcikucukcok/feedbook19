What do you do when someone comes along in your life with a chance for love? Especially when you've never achieved a goal, always been alone, but have distractions to build the ego. Well, that's a situation that comes to Willsie, a young man from Shen-Mont whose dream is to be Musician of the Universe, a symbol of success and fame in the genres of music.

There's a song he wrote for this beautiful woman, Taylor. She claims to be a singer he met on one of his walks. She knows she's not from his world but barely remembers what her own world looks like.

Over time, Willsie and Taylor learn more about each other’s music, and they start working on a few recordings together. Yet it could stir up something deeper for Willsie when he's told by his Goddess that if what he feels isn't acted on or shared with Taylor, a curse could be put upon him, a curse that could stop any dream of Taylor or music from happening and uncover a dark past and present truth that could haunt or teach him things about Taylor forever. Will he succeed or fail?
