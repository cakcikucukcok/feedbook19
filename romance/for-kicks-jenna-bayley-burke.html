Breeze Cohen knows there's something missing from her life, but her career doesn't leave time for anything but retail business strategy. She has to prove herself by making sure the product launch for her company's cornerstone client, Nitrous, is a success. No way is she is she about to let live-for-the-moment Logan Chandler tarnish her professional reputation. Even if the former athlete is now Nitrous's poster boy and makes her heart pound like she's run a marathon.

After surviving a near-fatal accident, Logan doesn't want to waste a minute of life. It's meant to be lived, ravished, enjoyed--and there's no one he'd like to ravish more than Breeze. There's a fiery desire beneath her icy facade. He can feel it every time they touch.

Logan's ready to take her for a run on the wild side--but he'll have to catch her.
