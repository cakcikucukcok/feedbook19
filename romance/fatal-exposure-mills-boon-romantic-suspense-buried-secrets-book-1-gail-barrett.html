Silence is her only protection

Pulitzer prize-winning photojournalist B.K. ("Brynn") Elliot chronicles Baltimore's grittier side with her lens–a talent cultivated from her years as a teenage runaway. A reclusive figure, Brynn lives under everyone's radar…until a photo from her past plunges her in the crosshairs of powerful enemies.

Detective Parker McCall has devoted fifteen years trying to solve his brother's murder, and with the release of a photo implicating Brynn as a potential suspect, he feels close to finding justice.

Determined to get answers, Parker must ignore the inexplicable attraction he feels for the haunted beauty in the photo. And Brynn must decide if Parker will protect her or betray her in his hunt for a killer.
