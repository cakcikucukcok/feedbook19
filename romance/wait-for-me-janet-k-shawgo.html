December 7th, 1941, America is at war. Many will go to fight, to heal and to report. The Whites’ and Bowens’ will be there.

 

Susan Bowen, nurse and great niece to Sarah Bowen will answer the call of her country. She will become a Task Nurse and care for the wounded on battlefields far away from Waynesboro Georgia. In another part of the world she finds someone to care for, maybe to love. Is it chance or fate?

 

Larry White, young, handsome and working his dream job as a reporter in Honolulu. Among the death and destruction at Pearl Harbor he is saved by a beautiful woman. He’s unsure if she was truly there or was it just a dream. Larry is determined to make a difference in the war by reporting it, just like his great uncle, Samuel White.

 

Deelyn Johns, pilot, mechanic and dreamer. The sky is her play ground. When the war begins Deelyn searches for a place, somewhere she can be of the greatest help. After all she can fly a plane!

 

They know the danger and face it; perhaps souls lost will find one another.
