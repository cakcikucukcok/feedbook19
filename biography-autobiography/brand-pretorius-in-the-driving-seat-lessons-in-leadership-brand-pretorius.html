Brand Pretorius knows what it means to be a leader: he was at the head of Toyota South Africa during its heyday, and took over as chief executive at the McCarthy Group. He knows that leadership means making hard and unpopular decision with an eye on the long term. He writes about his four decades in business and the lessons he learnt, illustrating his lessons on leadership with tales from his highly regarded career in motoring. This book gives a rare insight into what it means to do business in South Africa, and how a South African business leader needs to navigate our country's complexities.
