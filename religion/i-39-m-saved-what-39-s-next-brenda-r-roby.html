In Im SavedWhats Next? youll discover that the next step in salvation is to build a relationship with God. You have been living your life by your standards and by your strength. Its time to relinquish your way of doing things for Hisit works! In these last days, we must differentiate ourselves by operating in true love. Jesus said that people will know that we are His disciples by our love. By building a relationship with God, youll be able to walk out the word in your life with power, have joy unspeakable, and have better relationships with those around you.

Brenda Roby gives you 10 keys to begin building a relationship with Christ. These keys will revolutionize your life as you submit to Him. The 10 keys that will enable you to see life from a different perspective are:

Love God

Trust His plan

Agree with His word

Devote yourself to Him

Submit to the mission

Humble yourself

Ask Everyday

Meditate and Apply the Word

Walk by the Spirit

Be Persistent

By receiving Jesus Christ as your Savior and Lord, you have been given the ability and the wherewithal to transform your life day by day. Building a relationship with the Almighty God enhances the way you treat others by giving you more patience, understanding, and compassion; thereby, making your relationships and the people in them better. You are now savedbegin building your new life today!
