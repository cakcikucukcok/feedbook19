If youre a Christian who has read the Bible over the course of a year, congratulations! But what do you do next if you seek a more in-depth understanding of Scripture?

The next logical step is to go back to the Biblethis time at a slower paceand study key sections of God's word in greater depth. Author Brenton Cox invites you to spend a year with him studying one of the greatest books of the Bible: Psalms.

A Year in the Psalms is a daily devotional book designed to share the message of the psalms over the course of a year, delivered one psalm per week. It is an exploration of life lessons that will show you how to have a fresh start in life, how to better listen to God, and how, with Gods help, to overcome your fears.
