This book contains an assorted collection of short messages, inspired primarily by the Bible, and by what many have written and preached on various Biblical topics. The messages present the authors perspective of the profound truth in the teachings of Jesus Christ, as applied to life situations. It is believed that the essays would provoke the reader to undertake indepth study of the Bible. At the end of it all, it is hoped that the reader will find his or her own way of walking with Jesus. It is further hoped that this collection of essays would be used as a basis for devotions, group Bible studies and as outlines for topical sermons. Finally, this book is all about Jesus Christ, God the Son and is presented with humility for His glory.
