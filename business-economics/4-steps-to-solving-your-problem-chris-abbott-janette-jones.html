“4 Steps to Solving Your Problem” is a clear and concise building-block method to assist in the development of your troubleshooting and problem resolution skills. It doesn’t matter if you are an auto technician or a computer specialist, new to the service industry, a seasoned professional or a do-it-yourselfer, this book provides you with the tools and knowledge you need to troubleshoot and resolve any problem you encounter. The author, Chris Abbott, developed this method throughout his 25 years in the computer industry and quickly discovered his methods were universal and could be utilized in any industry or situation. Throughout this book you will discover the one common method all successful service providers use to resolve any and all problems they face.
