Imagine, if you had to put a 1000-piece puzzle together without so much as a glance at the big picture. Wouldn’t that be a discouraging task?

That is how many people find life. Millions of folks are struggling to locate satisfactory answers to life’s big questions. They wonder, “running around on this globe trying to have fun the best way to spend our few years of live existence?”

“There are many different explanations for our role on earth and how we arrived here. But who is correct? Does anybody possess an accurate picture so we can complete life’s puzzle?

Yes, with a humble thoroughness and confidence, Hurst leads the reader of this work on a tour of the box top to life’s puzzle.
