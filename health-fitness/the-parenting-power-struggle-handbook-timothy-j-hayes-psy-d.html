Parenting Power Struggle Handbook: How to Get Out of Power Struggles NOW and Avoid Them in the Future is the latest book from Timothy J. Hayes, Psy. D.

Dr. Hayes uses his years of experience to help you become a better parent and provide for a happier family life.

Question: Who should read this handbook?

Answer

Anyone who wants to get more of what they can out of life, with less frustration.

Anyone who is frequently angry and frustrated because other people don't do what one wants them to do.

Anyone who has children.

Anyone who feels they are "stuck" in a situation or relationship.

Anyone who feels they did not have good parenting role models.

Anyone who wants to be a better parent.

Anyone who does not like the way their children are acting.

Question: What will I learn by reading this book?

Answer

You will learn the single most useful thing I have learned in twenty-nine years of doing therapy. You will learn how to identify a power struggle.

You will learn how to get out of power struggle.

You will learn how to build your credibility.

You will learn how to create a family structure and house rules that can lead to a healthier, happier family.

You will learn how to use things you can control to make your life more safe, more sane, and more gratifying.
