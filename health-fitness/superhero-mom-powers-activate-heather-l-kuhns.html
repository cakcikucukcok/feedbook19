Superhero Mom Powers Activate  is an inspirational book meant for busy moms who are looking for ways to get it all done in their crazy busy worlds. Author Heather Kuhns takes you through her own journey with her three active children, but also offers pearls of wisdom for how other moms can connect. Heather’s hope is that other moms will reflect on their own busy lives and understand that they can get it all done, even in the busiest of worlds. 

A different superhero mom power sets the stage for each chapter, telling the stories of Heather’s three children, who are always going in different directions. Superhero Mom Powers Activate takes you through the zany tales of an actor, a gymnast, and a toddler who just goes along for the ride, and all the craziness. 

And while the kid stories may not be the same, the need for moms to do it all—and be it all—is one that is surely relatable.
