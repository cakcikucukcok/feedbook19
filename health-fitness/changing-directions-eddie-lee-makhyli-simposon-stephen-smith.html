Eddie and Makhyli had a perfect relationship; they did everything together. Many people admired their connection and felt inspired by their closeness. They were not only mother and daughter, but artist and manager that worked effortlessly together to get Makhyli's career off the ground. Then Makhyli turned 13 years old and everything seemed to change in the blink of an eye.

Mothers and daughters of all circumstances and backgrounds may find themselves struggling with issues they are not prepared to handle in a positive way during the pre-teen and teen years. Challenges may seem to blindside both mother and daughter and endanger a once loving and compassionate relationship. With Makhyli's career in jeopardy and the threat of growing apart, Eddie and Makhyli came up with ways to strengthen their bond and preserve both their relationship and Makhyli's career.

Changing Directions offers an account of their challenges and provides fun activities to do together such that will strengthen bonds and create lasting memories. The book includes a ton of writing prompts to get conversations going in a safe way and a mother-daughter journal to share thoughts and ideas. Changing Directions also doubles as a keepsake and can be shared through generations, passing along thoughts, information, and new traditions. 

If you are ready to take a step closer towards a stronger and deeper relationship with your mother or daughter, this book will help you start today!
