So are you beating your head around trying to figure out whether your partner truly loves you or not? You see we live in the day and age where people might say something and mean the exact opposite of it. Therefore you partner might tell you that he/she loves you but how can you be ever sure unless you know for sure that they actually mean it? There are some mind blowing ways using which you can easily figure out whether your mate's love towards you is true or not. Read on to discover how you can find out whether your partner truly loves you or not...

Are they afraid to lose you? - Does your mate always keep asking you whether you will be faithful towards them or not? You see when the love is true your mate will always have the fear that they might lose you some day. With the feelings of extreme joy there will always be feelings of insecurity.

He/She gives you top priority- Does it seem like that your mate treats you like the most important person of their life? Do they always end up doing things for you even when they tend to have more important things in their life they have to deal with? When the love is true you will be the most important thing in your partner's life and they would do almost anything to fulfill your needs and see you happy.

Kelechi Ogbonna
