Stretching Your Way leaves all other muscle stretching systems in the Stone Age in terms of comfort, pleasure, and health. Toss out those stretches that drain you! Where other stretching systems age you as they stress you physically and emotionally, Stretching Your Way rejuvenates you as you stretch in complete harmony with your natural instincts. You work with nature and not against it. Stretching Your Way is truly no work and all play and effortless in every way. Another wonderful aspect of Stretching Your Way is its efficiency-you can stretch while engaged in other activities and many times turn a tedious task into pure bliss. Larry Piller shows you how to bring magic into your stretches-and encourages you to do them only when you want. Yes, forget the must-do schedules! His new way of stretching frees you from boring routines and awkward stretches. Stretching Your Way is a treasure trove of the world's easiest stretches that will work like magic for you every time because each works in perfect blissful harmony with you emotionally and physically.
