Look Up! Birds and Other Natural Wonders Just Outside Your Window is a collection of uplifting yet scientifically-informed nature essays about experiencing nature in an affordable, accessible way.  It highlights simple, practical ways to tune in to birds, nature, light, peace and weather – often in one's own back yard. 

This book will inspire readers to savor the simple pleasures of life by experiencing the nature that is all around us – even in urban environments where most of us live.  Doing so is a powerful antidote to our materialistic, electronic screen-addicted society.  It will lead to a greater understanding and appreciation of our natural world, which in turn will lead to its conservation.  

It also explores how we can derive psychological benefits from experiencing nature -- how it can help us live happier, more fulfilled and rewarding lives.  Look Up! displays the vibrant beauty of the natural world in all of its splendor through 40 stunning full-color photos.

The book is dedicated to one of America's early conservationists, the author's great grandfather, Jens Jensen, who is featured in several of the book's essays.  The content was inspired by the author's work as a nature educator, tour guide and conservation nonprofit manager.  The book's content is unique in that it covers a wide array of nature appreciation topics, including the importance of conservation and how interacting with nature enhances our well-being.  Most importantly, it does so in a joyful and at times,a humorous tone.
